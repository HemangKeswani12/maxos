# Beard Architecture & Development

## Face Shape Principles

The beard should counteract face shape weaknesses and create an oval illusion:

| Face Shape | Beard Strategy |
|-----------|---------------|
| Round | Sharp, squared-off beard lines. Longer on chin to add length. Keep cheeks close. |
| Long/Narrow | Full sides, avoid goatee (elongates further). Keep chin beard shorter. |
| Square/Angular | Rounded neckline. Slightly fuller sides soften angles. |
| Triangular (wide jaw) | Volume on upper face, trim lower. |
| Weak/Recessed chin | Longer beard at chin projects and adds definition. |

## Critical Lines

### Neckline
The neckline is the most impactful and most commonly miscut feature.

**Correct position:** Two finger-widths above the Adam's apple. Follow the natural line of the neck, curving up toward each ear.

**Mistake:** Cutting too high creates a neckbeard look and makes the jaw appear smaller. When in doubt, cut lower than you think.

### Cheek Line
Natural or trimmed 1–2mm above the natural hair growth line. A clean cheek line makes the beard look intentional. Shaving the cheeks too aggressively makes the beard look disconnected.

## Beard Growth Protocol

### Minoxidil for Patchy Beard Growth
The most evidence-based intervention for increasing beard density.

- **Application:** Topical minoxidil 5%, twice daily to cheeks and affected areas
- **Timeline:** 16–24 weeks for visible density improvement
- **Mechanism:** Extends anagen (growth) phase, vasodilation, directly stimulates follicle activity
- **Note:** Results are only maintained with continued use. Discontinuation reverses gains.
- **Side effects:** Dryness at application site (manage with moisturiser). Systemic absorption is minimal.

### Dermarolling (0.5mm) for Beard
Once per week on beard area before minoxidil. Creates micro-channels, significantly increases absorption. Evidence: moderate.

## Maintenance

**Tools:**
- Trimmer with adjustable guard
- Beard scissors for detail
- Fine-tooth comb for alignment
- Single-blade razor for neckline and cheek lines

**Cadence:**
- Neckline and cheek lines: Every 3–4 days
- Length maintenance: Weekly
- Beard oil: Daily (jojoba or argan base) — prevents brittle hairs and dry skin underneath

**Beard comb:** Start from the bottom, detangle upward. Distributes oil evenly. Trains growth direction.

## Beard Conditioning

- Beard wash (not regular shampoo) 2–3×/week — face skin has different lipid balance
- Beard balm (vs oil): Better for longer beards, provides hold and conditioning simultaneously
- Exfoliate skin under beard weekly to prevent folliculitis and ingrown hairs
