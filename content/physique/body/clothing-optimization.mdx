# Clothing for Body Optimization

## Hierarchy

**Fit > Fabric > Brand > Price**

A perfectly-fitted $40 shirt outperforms a $400 designer shirt that doesn't fit. Most people invert this hierarchy.

## The Fit Rules

### Shoulders
The shoulder seam must sit at the exact edge of your shoulder bone. Test by raising your arm:
- Seam creases and pulls = too small
- Seam droops onto upper arm = too large
- This is the most critical fit point — cannot be tailored effectively

### Chest/Torso
1–2cm of space from armpit to shirt body when arms are lowered. No more. The shirt should skim the torso, not tent or compress.

### Sleeves
Shirt sleeves: Cuff hits the wrist bone. 1–1.5cm of cuff visible below jacket sleeve.
T-shirt: Sleeve hem at mid-bicep.

### Trousers
- Waist: Sits at natural waist without a belt being required to hold them up
- Seat: No excess fabric bunching
- Thigh: Enough room to move without restriction
- Break: Slim trousers should have a clean break or no break at the shoe

**Everything above the shoulder seam can be tailored inexpensively:** Chest taken in, sleeves shortened, trousers hemmed.

## V-Taper Clothing Strategy

**Tops:**
- Light colours on top, dark on bottom = more visual width at shoulders, less at hips
- Structured shoulders (slight padding) on shirts/blazers
- Fit through the torso to show shape

**Avoid:**
- Baggy tops (hides shoulder width and creates bulk without shape)
- Vertical logos or graphics that go down the centre (narrows appearance)
- High collar with short neck (emphasises short neck)

**Suits and blazers:** One of the most effective V-taper tools available. A well-fitted single-breasted, two-button blazer creates strong shoulder line.

## Height Optimization

- **Chelsea or dealer boots with stacked heel (3–4cm):** Most natural-looking height increase
- **Monochromatic dressing:** Same colour head-to-toe elongates the silhouette
- **High-rise trousers:** Raise the visual waist line
- **Avoid:** Horizontal waist bands, tucked-out shirts that break the line

## Colour Principles

| Goal | Strategy |
|------|---------|
| Look taller | Monochrome, vertical elements, high waist |
| Look broader | Light top, shoulder structure |
| Look leaner | Dark, fitted, minimal pattern |
| Look powerful | Contrast collar (white shirt, dark suit) |

## Investment Priority

1. **Well-fitted chinos or trousers** — versatile foundation
2. **White Oxford shirt (tailored)** — the most reliable piece in any wardrobe
3. **Dark jeans with straight/slim fit** — replace cargo shorts and baggy jeans
4. **Chelsea boots** — elevate any casual outfit, provide height
5. **Structured blazer** — transforms casual outfits, essential for formal
