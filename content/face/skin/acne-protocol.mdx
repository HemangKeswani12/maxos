# Acne & Texture Elimination Protocol

## Mechanism

Acne is a multi-factorial disease: excess sebum, *C. acnes* colonization, follicular hyperkeratinization, and inflammatory cascade. Effective treatment targets ≥2 of these simultaneously.

## Active Ingredient Stack

### Morning
1. Gentle cleanser (pH 4.5-6.5)
2. Niacinamide 10% — sebum regulation, anti-inflammatory
3. SPF 50+ — mandatory. UV worsens PIH.

### Evening
1. Oil cleanser (double cleanse if wearing SPF)
2. Salicylic acid 2% (BHA) — 4x/week, comedolytic
3. Tretinoin 0.025% — 3x/week initially, titrate to nightly over 8 weeks
4. Ceramide moisturizer — barrier repair

## Protocol Notes

- **Do not** combine benzoyl peroxide with tretinoin (oxidizes retinol)
- **Purging** is expected in weeks 2-6 with tretinoin. Not a reaction — increased cell turnover
- Dietary elimination trial: Remove dairy and high-GI foods for 4 weeks. Track with photos.
