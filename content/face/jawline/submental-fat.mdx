# Submental Fat Reduction

## Mechanism

Submental fat (the fat beneath the chin and jaw) behaves like all other body fat: it can only be reduced through systemic energy deficit. There is no exercise, cream, or massage that selectively reduces it.

However, it is worth understanding that:
1. **Face fat distribution is genetic.** Some people store proportionally more fat in their face.
2. **Submental fat often responds early** to caloric deficit — faces tend to lean out faster than the trunk.
3. **Posture dramatically affects appearance** — forward head posture visually compresses this region.

## Body Fat Reduction

**Target body fat % for visible jawline:**
- Males: 12–15%
- Females: 18–22%

Protocol: See Body Fat Reduction protocol for full detail.

Key variables:
- 300–500 kcal deficit
- 2.0–2.4g/kg protein to preserve muscle
- 8–12 weeks to visible facial change

## Posture Correction

Forward head posture physically compresses the submental region, creating a visual double chin even at low body fat. This is often the primary cause in lean individuals.

Fix: Forward Head Posture protocol. Results visible in 4–8 weeks.

## Water Retention

High sodium intake causes facial bloating, which amplifies the appearance of submental fat. Protocol for events/photos:
- 48h prior: Reduce sodium below 1500mg/day
- Increase water to 3–4L/day (paradoxically reduces retention)
- Avoid alcohol (major bloating contributor)

## Medical Options

**Kybella (deoxycholic acid):**
- Injected directly into submental fat
- Permanently destroys fat cells via detergent mechanism
- 2–4 sessions typically required
- Results permanent (fat cells don't regenerate)
- Side effects: Significant swelling for 1–3 weeks, numbness

**Coolsculpting/Cryolipolysis:**
- Non-invasive fat cell destruction via controlled cooling
- Less effective than Kybella for submental region
- Some risk of paradoxical adipose hyperplasia (rare but permanent worsening)

**Recommendation:** Exhaust dietary and posture approaches first. Medical interventions should be last resort.
